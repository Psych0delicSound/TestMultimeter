### Ohm law

R - Resistance (Ohm Ω)
I|A - Current Strength (Ampere)
U|V - Voltage (Volt)
P - Power

```
P = V * A
P = A * A * R
P = V * V / R

V = A * R
V = P / A
V = SqrRt(P * R)

A = V / R
A = P / V
A = SqrRt(P / R)

R = V / A
R = P / (A * A)
R = V * V / P
```
