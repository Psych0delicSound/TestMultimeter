### Ohm law

R - Resistance (Ohm Ω)
I|A - Current Strength (Ampere)
U|V - Voltage (Volt)
P - Power

```
P = V * A
P = A * A * R
P = V * V / R

V = A * R
A = V / R
R = V / A
```
